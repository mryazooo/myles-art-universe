About Myles

Myles is a young artist who enjoys drawing comic book heroes, villains and original characters. He experiments with pencil, ink and markers, focusing on anatomy, lighting and textures.

This site collects some of his favourite pieces and studies so far. As his style evolves and new work is created, the gallery and sketchbook sections will continue to grow.

Outside of drawing, Myles is often reading, watching or playing stories that inspire new ideas for characters and scenes.
